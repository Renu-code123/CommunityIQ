# 📘 CommunityIQ User Manual & Navigation Guide

Welcome to **CommunityIQ**, your centralized intelligence and automation platform. This guide will walk you through how to navigate the website, use its core features, and get the most out of your multi-agent system.

---

## 🚪 1. Getting Started (Login & Landing Page)

When you first visit CommunityIQ, you will see the **Landing Page**. 
- To access the platform, click the **"Launch Demo"** or **"Login"** button.
- You will be taken to the Authentication gateway. Enter your credentials or continue as a Demo Administrator.
- Once logged in, you will always land on your **Dashboard**, which serves as your command center.

---

## 🧭 2. Main Navigation Menu

The platform is divided into several specialized modules accessible from the sidebar navigation menu. Here is a detailed breakdown of each section:

### 🏠 Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
**What it is:** Your high-level overview.
**How to use it:** 
- View at-a-glance metrics (e.g., active agents, system health).
- Check recent alerts and quick summaries of community data.
- Use this as your starting point every day to see what needs immediate attention.

### 💬 Chat Assistant (`/chat`)
**What it is:** Your direct interface with the AI.
**How to use it:**
- Type questions or commands into the chat box.
- Ask the AI to summarize documents, pull recent predictions, or explain alerts.
- The chat assistant can pull data from all other modules to give you comprehensive answers.

### 🤖 Agents Workflow (`/agents`)
**What it is:** The control room for your specialized AI agents.
**How to use it:**
- Monitor the status of your 6 specialized agents.
- See real-time logs of tasks they are coordinating and executing.
- Pause, restart, or reconfigure agent responsibilities.

### 📊 Analytics & Insights
- **Analytics (`/analytics`):** View historical data trends, charts, and deep dives into community metrics. You can filter by date ranges and export data.
- **Predictions (`/predictions`):** Access the predictive AI models (e.g., traffic forecasting, flood warnings). It shows you what is *likely* to happen in the future based on past data.
- **Recommendations (`/recommendations`):** Actionable advice generated by the AI based on the analytics. (e.g., "Increase energy grid capacity in Sector 4").

### 🚨 Alert Center (`/alerts`)
**What it is:** The hub for all critical notifications and anomalies.
**How to use it:**
- View active alerts (categorized by severity: Low, Medium, High, Critical).
- Click on an alert to see its source, affected area, and AI-suggested mitigation steps.
- Acknowledge or resolve alerts to remove them from the active queue.

### 🗺️ Smart City Map (`/map`)
**What it is:** A geospatial view of your data.
**How to use it:**
- View a visual map with pinned alerts, traffic data, and infrastructure status.
- Zoom in on specific sectors to see localized data points and agent activities.

### 📄 Document & Report Management
- **Document Center (`/documents`):** Upload, store, and manage PDFs, CSVs, and policies. The AI can read these to inform its decisions.
- **Report Generator (`/reports`):** Create custom, downloadable reports. Select your data sources, date range, and let the AI compile a professional summary for stakeholders.

### ⚙️ System Configuration
- **Data Sources (`/sources`):** Manage API connections and external data feeds (IoT sensors, weather APIs, etc.).
- **Automation Builder (`/automation`):** Create "If This, Then That" rules. For example: *If a flood warning is triggered, automatically send an email to the emergency response team.*
- **Settings (`/settings`):** Manage your user profile, team permissions, notification preferences, and system themes.

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for Success

1. **Rely on the Chat Assistant:** If you don't know where to find something, just go to the **Chat Assistant** and ask, "Where can I find the latest traffic prediction?"
2. **Review Alerts First:** Always check the **Alert Center** at the start of your session to address high-priority anomalies.
3. **Automate Repetitive Tasks:** Use the **Automation Builder** to save time on routine monitoring. Let the agents do the heavy lifting!
